# ShopNest Store Power BI Capstone

## Project Overview

The ShopNest Store Power BI Capstone is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project that analyzes e-commerce sales, customer behavior, payment trends, and delivery performance using Power BI. The project integrates multiple datasets to create interactive dashboards that help stakeholders monitor KPIs and make data-driven business decisions.

---

## Business Problem

E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of transactional and customer data, making it difficult to:

- Monitor sales performance across regions and product categories.
- Identify delayed deliveries and operational bottlenecks.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior and payment preferences.
- Track key business KPIs from a centralized dashboard.

---

## Project Objective

- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for business performance monitoring.
- Analyze sales, revenue, customer behavior, and delivery efficiency.
- Identify high-performing products and customer segments.
- Generate actionable insights to improve profitability and customer satisfaction.

---

## Key Analysis

### Sales & Revenue Analysis

- Identified top-performing product categories.
- Evaluated monthly and yearly revenue trends.
- Compared state-wise sales performance.

### Order & Delivery Performance

- Analyzed delayed versus on-time deliveries.
- Evaluated delivery efficiency by category.
- Identified operational bottlenecks.

### Customer & Payment Insights

- Examined customer purchasing behavior.
- Analyzed preferred payment methods.
- Identified highest and lowest-rated products.
- Evaluated customer acquisition and revenue trends.

---

## Business Insights

- Top product categories generated the highest revenue.
- Delivery delays were concentrated in specific product categories.
- Customer purchasing behavior varied across regions and seasons.
- Credit cards were the dominant payment method.
- Seasonal demand significantly influenced sales performance.

---

## Recommendations

- Improve logistics for delayed product categories.
- Optimize inventory during peak sales periods.
- Promote alternative payment methods.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions.
- Improve underperforming product categories to increase revenue.

---

## Outcomes

- Built an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard for end-to-end business monitoring.
- Improved visibility into sales, customer behavior, and delivery performance.
- Enabled data-driven decision-making using KPI dashboards and interactive reports.
